The Best Disney's Movies According to Mei

My top 3 Disney's movies of all-time.

Turning Red

Turning Red is an upcoming animated film produced by Pixar Animation Studios for Walt Disney Pictures. The story follows a 13-year-old girl named Mei Lee who discovers that whenever she gets too excited, she transforms into a giant red panda.

Encanto

Encanto is an animated musical fantasy film produced by Walt Disney Animation Studios. The story is set in Colombia and revolves around a magical family who live in a magical house. The protagonist, Mirabel, is the only family member without magical powers, and she sets out to discover the true magic within herself.

Lightyear

Lightyear is an upcoming animated science fiction film produced by Pixar Animation Studios for Walt Disney Pictures. It serves as a spin-off/prequel to the Toy Story franchise, focusing on the origin story of Buzz Lightyear, the character who inspired the toy.
